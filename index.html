Depth of Knowledge

Understanding DOK Levels

The EOPA uses three levels of thinking. Knowing the difference helps you study smarter — not just memorize facts.

1
DOK Level 1
Recall & Reproduce
Remember facts, definitions, and basic procedures. These questions test whether you know the content.
"What does RICE stand for?" · "Which ligament does valgus stress test?" · "Define hyponatremia."
2
DOK Level 2
Skills & Concepts
Apply knowledge, compare ideas, explain how or why something works. These go beyond simple recall.
"Why does cryotherapy reduce swelling?" · "How does Wolff's Law apply to rehab?" · "What causes hyponatremia?"
3
DOK Level 3
Strategic Thinking
Analyze scenarios, evaluate options, draw conclusions. The hardest questions — and worth the most on the exam.
"Which finding BEST indicates a Grade III ACL tear?" · "An athlete presents with… what is the MOST appropriate action?"
